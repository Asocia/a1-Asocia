Rubik's Cube ,also known as magic cube, is a 3-D combination puzzle invented in 1974 by Hungarian sculptor and professor of architecture Erno Rubik. It is consisting of moving surface and commonly made of plastic. Basically, there are 4 different types offered to the market: -2x2x2 Pocket Cube -3x3x3 Standard Cube -4x4x4 Rubik's Revenge -5x5x5 Professor's Cube In the standard 3x3x3 model known as "Rubik's Cube", there are 9 squares on each faces. Since the cube has 6 faces there are 54 square in a standard cube. And its volume is 26 unit cube except the little cube which cannot be seen in the centre. Usually all the faces are colored with a different color. And when you solve the puzzle each face of the cube consists of one color as you can see the picture below. At first the puzzle called "Magic Cube" by its creator. Then in 1980, it is called "Rubik's Cube". And in May of the same year distribution started all over the world. It sold 300 million until 2007 and became the most popular toy in the world.
